Filevine founder Ryan Anderson announces the launch of LOIS console, an AI-powered legal software that autonomously manages legal matters by reading documents, surfacing facts with source citations, and performing tasks like drafting documents and updating records. The product represents a decade-long vision to eliminate reliance on luck in case management and empower lawyers to focus on strategy rather than manual document review.
